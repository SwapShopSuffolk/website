What We Do

four ways we make a difference

From weekly pop-ups to repair workshops and community partnerships, we're building a circular fashion movement in Lowestoft. Every activity, service and collaboration contributes to our mission: keeping clothes in circulation and out of landfill.

01
Weekly Swap Shop

A weekly pop-up in Lowestoft where the community can donate, exchange, or purchase quality clothing, shoes and accessories at little to no cost — fostering community involvement and a circular economy. Drop by on Wednesdays or Fridays to find your next favourite piece.

02
Repairs & Collaborations

In-house repairs for damaged donations plus a repair service for the public. Items beyond repair are passed to local artists, charities and small businesses for creative repurposing — nothing goes to waste.

03
Awareness & Education

We run sustainability talks, workshops and share educational content on social media — offering sustainable living advice, repair skills and community tips. Knowledge is power in the fight against fast fashion.

04
Local Partnerships

We collaborate with local businesses and organisations to provide free clothing, repairs and garment care advice to people who are homeless, returning to work, or on low incomes. Supporting our most vulnerable community members is at the heart of everything we do.
