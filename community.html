Who We Serve

built for our community

Swap Shop Suffolk was founded to serve everyone in Lowestoft and across Suffolk who deserves access to quality clothing and sustainable choices — regardless of their income. We believe that sustainable fashion should never be a luxury.

Community Groups

we serve everyone

Our community includes:

Low-income households
Working class families
People returning to work
Those experiencing homelessness
Local artists & makers
Eco-conscious shoppers
Anyone with clothes to share
